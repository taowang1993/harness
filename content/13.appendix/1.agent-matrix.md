## One-Line Summary

- **Codex CLI**: strongest built-in containment, shell-first.
- **Claude Code**: strongest polished app-layer product behavior.
- **OpenCode**: strongest open-source coding-product surface.
- **Pi**: strongest low-level extensibility surface.
- **Hermes**: strongest operational breadth across chat, gateway, plugins, and execution backends.
